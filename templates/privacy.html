Privacy Policy for TeamEQ

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TeamEQ ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our sports team management application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Username: Used for login and identification within your league
  • Password: Stored securely using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt hashing)
  • Email address: Not currently collected

2.2 Game Data

  • Player names: Names you create for your team roster
  • Match results: Scores, points, and game outcomes you submit
  • Statistics: Calculated performance metrics based on match results
  • League information: Game names, invite codes, and league memberships

2.3 Technical Data

  • Session data: Temporary cookies to keep you logged in
  • IP address: Used for rate limiting and security purposes
  • Device information: Browser type and operating system (from standard HTTP headers)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely for the following purposes:

  • App functionality: To provide team balancing, statistics tracking, and league management
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and maintain your login session
  • Security: To prevent abuse, spam, and unauthorized access
  • Service improvement: To fix bugs and improve app performance

4. How We Store Your Data

  • Database: Your data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Render.com
  • Encryption: All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted connections)
  • Password security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text
  • Backups: Regular automated backups are maintained for data recovery

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your league members: Player names, statistics, and match results are visible to other members of your league
  • Service providers: Render.com (hosting), as required to operate the service
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: All data is permanently deleted within 48 hours of account deletion request
  • Backups: Deleted data may persist in backups for up to 30 days before permanent removal

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Correction: Update your username or league information through the app
  • Deletion: Request account deletion at any time (see section 8)
  • Data portability: Export your league data (feature coming soon)

8. Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Visit our Account Deletion page for instructions.

What gets deleted:

  • Your user account and login credentials
  • All player profiles you created
  • All match results and statistics
  • League memberships
  • All associated game data

Timeline: Your data will be permanently deleted within 48 hours of your request.

9. Children's Privacy

TeamEQ is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Cookies and Tracking

  • Session cookies: Essential for login functionality (expires after 30 minutes of inactivity)
  • No tracking cookies: We do not use advertising or analytics cookies
  • No third-party trackers: No Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar services

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on servers located in the United States (Render.com infrastructure). By using TeamEQ, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States.

12. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Bcrypt password hashing
  • Rate limiting to prevent brute force attacks
  • CSRF protection against cross-site attacks
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of TeamEQ after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@teameq.app (or your actual email)
  • Website: https://teams-7g57.onrender.com

15. Compliance

This privacy policy is designed to comply with:

  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - for EU users
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - for California users
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
